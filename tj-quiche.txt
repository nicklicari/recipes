Ingredients:

- 1 frozen Trader Joe's quiche

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
 
 2. Remove the quiche from the wrapper and cardboard, and place on a baking pan.
 
 3. Bake 24 minutes at 400 degrees F.

 4. Let sit for 1 minute, then confirm internal temperature of quiche is 165 degrees F.
